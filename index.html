Preference, Not Skill

Before you begin, it's important to understand what we mean by "preference." Think about signing your name.

You can likely write with both hands, but you have a natural preference for one. Writing with your preferred hand is smooth, easy, and comfortable. Writing with the other hand feels awkward, slow, and takes more concentration, even if you practice.
